<div style="text-align: justify;">
<p>I am currently a R&D engineer at Valeo and a member of the <a href="https://astra-team.github.io/">ASTRA team</a>, a joint Inria–Valeo research team focused on intelligent transportation systems and automated driving. I conduct research on deep reinforcement learning, decision and trajectory planning for autonomous vehicles, and intelligent transportation systems. I also co-supervise doctoral research, notably that of Mr. Islem Kobbi. </p>
</div>

<div style="text-align: justify;">
<p>In 2022, I received my PhD degree in network, information and communication from <a href="https://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/ecoles-doctorales/sciences-et-technologies-de-linformation-et-de-la-communication-stic">Université Paris-Saclay, CentraleSupélec</a>, under the supervision of <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=fr&user=JR5JJEjJsIwC">Dr. Salah E. Elayoubi</a> and <a href="https://sites.google.com/site/vineethshome/">Dr. Vineeth S. Varma</a> at <a href="https://l2s.centralesupelec.fr/">Laboratoire des signaux et systèmes (L2S)</a>, France. My PhD thesis is entitled <a href="https://tel.archives-ouvertes.fr/tel-03520817">Robust control of platooning systems over imperfect wireless channels</a>.</p>
</div>

<div style="text-align: justify;">
<p>In 2018, I received a MSc degree in electrical engineering with emphasis in control and automation from <a href="https://www.fee.unicamp.br/node/26?language=en">Universidade Estadual de Campinas (UNICAMP)</a>, Brazil, under the supervision of <a href="https://scholar.google.com.br/citations?user=Sb_7MekAAAAJ&hl=pt-BR">Dr. Jose C. Geromel</a> and <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=0nK5I5kAAAAJ&hl=fr">Dr. Gabriela W. Gabriel</a>. </div>

My research interests include:
* Deep Reinforcement Learning
* Decision and Trajectory Planning for Autonomous Vehicles
* Vehicle Platooning and Cooperative Driving
* Control Theory and Linear Matrix Inequalities (LMI)
* Joint Communication and Control Systems
